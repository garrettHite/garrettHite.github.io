NEON

Neon is a Virtual Reality game that I created using the Unity engine for an Intro to V.R. class at Cu Boulder.

Neon is a simple game where the player must defend the orb in the center of the stage from the dragon flying above using a swinging attack, the sword, and a shooting attack, the gun. The dragon will use two different attacks against the player. The first being a spread of fire balls that come out of the dragon’s mouth. The second being a small barrage of missiles that shoot out of the dragon’s back. These projectiles are color coded to be either red or blue which correlate to the detail coloring of the players weapons. The blue blade of the sword indicates that it destroys blue projectiles while the red core of the gun indicates it destroys red projectiles. The player loses the game when the orb is destroyed and wins the game if they can defend the orb for duration of the dragon’s attacks.
