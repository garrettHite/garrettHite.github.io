About Me

Through games, I have grown to love the ways in which coding and computer science is an endless world of possibility and creativity.

EDUCATION

I am a student at The University of Colorado Boulder pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science. I have taken many Computer Science courses such as Data Structures, Algorithms, Computer Graphics, Intro to Virtual Reality, and Software Development that have helped me hone my skills in programming and software design.
